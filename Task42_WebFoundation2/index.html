Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flours

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  5. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  6. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown.
